Waleed Arshad

Waleed Arshad is a mobile technologist, a core developer and one of the founders of Flutter Karachi (a Flutter community in Pakistan). He mainly focuses on developing cross-platform mobile applications on Flutter and is always inclined towards consistently coping up with leading-edge technology orientations. He has been a part of IBM, TPS Worldwide and Karachi Electric, and is currently working in Karachi as a Senior Mobile Application Engineer at Unikrew Solutions.

He is a musician and a photographer by hobby and writes tech articles on Medium.
